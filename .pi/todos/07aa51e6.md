Applied designer feedback to the public-facing site styles.

Updated files:
- `app/styles/base.css`
- `app/styles/components.css`
- `app/styles/pages.css`

What changed:
- Raised dark theme secondary text contrast globally by brightening `--gray` and `--text-secondary`.
- Increased public nav legibility and exposed the mobile menu up to tablet widths (`max-width: 900px`).
- Increased sizes/contrast on homepage + apps pages for hero labels, subtitles, descriptions, rail items, stats labels, filters, cards, and footer links/copy.
- Added stronger phone-specific typography and spacing for the store hero, rail, buttons, and stats.
- Increased readability on feedback, roadmap, and changelog public pages by enlarging subtitles, controls, badges, card copy, metadata, and mobile layouts.

Intent:
- Preserve the overall dark / minimal aesthetic while making muted text readable on phones and non-Retina displays.
